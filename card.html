Goat

Goats were one of the first animals to be tamed by humans and have been used for milk, meat, fur, and skins across much of the world

  • Scientific Name: Capra aegagrus hircus
  • Favorite Food: Grass
  • Average Lifespan: 16 years
  • Habitat: Anywhere with food!

Goats are stout-bodied mammals with horns and cloven hooves.
